About Me

I'm an IAM architect and AI/ML engineer with over a decade building enterprise identity platforms — OAuth2/OIDC/SAML, MFA, directory integration, device trust, and risk-adaptive authentication — at Acceptto and SecureAuth, and now architecting a confidential, pre-launch integrated identity platform with agentic AI assurance.

I also build production-ready AI systems — LLMs, RAG, tool-calling agents, and classical ML — including fail-closed step-up MFA, eval-gated releases, and validation through CI. I focus on problems where IAM architecture, ML, and production engineering intersect.
